Turbidity

Turbidity is the haziness or cloudiness of a fluid caused by large number of individual particles that are generally inisible to the naked eye.

It is the measure of relative clarity of a liquid. Also it is an important indicator of the amount of suspended sediments in water which can not only have adverse effects on human life but also on aquatic animals.
